My Teaching Journey

I started teaching in 1995 at Westwood Regional Jr./Sr. High School. That makes this my 31st year in the teaching world.

In 2002, I left Westwood for an 18-year stint at Northern Highlands Regional High School until I left in 2020. From there, I did a quick stop at New Milford High School before joining the staff at Ridgewood in February 2023. I am very happy to be a part of the math department at RHS.

Teaching Focus

I have taught every subject in the math curriculum, with most of my career focused on AP Calculus and Honors Precalculus.
